Sleep Apnea Risk Assessment

আপনার স্লিপ অ্যাপনিয়ার ঝুঁকি জানুন — Answer 8 simple questions to find out your risk level. Takes less than 3 minutes.

ℹ️

STOP-BANG Questionnaire

This validated clinical tool is used worldwide to screen for obstructive sleep apnea risk. এটি একটি বৈজ্ঞানিক প্রশ্নপত্র যা আন্তর্জাতিকভাবে স্বীকৃত। Your results are not a medical diagnosis — please consult a specialist for formal evaluation.

Your Progress 0 of 8 answered

S
নাক ডাকা — Snoring
Do you snore loudly (louder than talking or loud enough to be heard through closed doors)?
T
ক্লান্তি — Tired
Do you often feel tired, fatigued, or sleepy during the daytime (such as falling asleep during driving)?
O
পর্যবেক্ষণ — Observed
Has anyone observed you stop breathing or choking/gasping during your sleep?
P
রক্তচাপ — Blood Pressure
Do you have or are you being treated for high blood pressure (hypertension)?
B
BMI — শরীরের ভর সূচক
Is your Body Mass Index (BMI) greater than 35 kg/m²? (Overweight/obese by clinical standard)
A
বয়স — Age
Are you older than 50 years of age?
N
গলার মাপ — Neck Circumference
Is your neck circumference greater than 40 cm (approximately 16 inches)?
G
লিঙ্গ — Gender
Are you male? (Males have a higher statistical risk of obstructive sleep apnea)

Please answer all 8 questions to see your result.

⚕️

Medical Disclaimer

This screening tool is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a medical diagnosis. এই স্ক্রিনিং টুলটি শুধুমাত্র তথ্যের জন্য। For a formal evaluation and treatment, please consult our specialists at SleepApneaBD.
